How It Works

What is BeReal Scavenger Hunt?

The BeReal Scavenger Hunt is a unique, interactive experience that combines the thrill of a scavenger hunt with the latest in augmented reality technology. Players receive real-time challenges and clues on their mobile devices, leading them on adventures through their city.

How to Play

1. Download the App

Get the BeReal Scavenger Hunt app on your phone.

2. Choose or Create a Hunt

Pick an existing hunt or design your own adventure.

3. Follow the Clues

Solve challenges and find hidden treasures.

4. Compete and Win

Challenge friends and earn points to win rewards.
